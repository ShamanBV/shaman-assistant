# Feature 119

Feature Name: Feature 119

The VeevaImages feature is a Shared builder capability that enables pharmaceutical marketers and content administrators to seamlessly access and incorporate approved brand and country-specific images directly from their client's Veeva Vault Digital Asset Management system into Shaman content authoring workflows. This integration requires additional configuration setup and allows marketing teams to maintain brand compliance by pulling pre-approved visual assets, product images, and promotional materials that have already gone through the medical, legal, and regulatory review process within Veeva Vault. Content creators and field teams can confidently use these vetted images in their Closed Loop Marketing presentations, Approved Email campaigns, and other promotional materials without concerns about regulatory compliance or brand guideline violations. The feature streamlines the asset management process by eliminating the need to manually download, store, and track approved images separately, while ensuring that only current, compliant visual content is available for use in pharmaceutical marketing communications and sales enablement materials.

## 1. What Shaman Is (Core Definition)

Shaman is a content authoring platform for the pharmaceutical industry, designed to create, localize, manage, and update approved multichannel marketing and medical content.

Shaman is a **guided self-service authoring platform** for end users such as brand marketers and digital managers, instead of agency designers and developers. This makes local pharma teams more agile, flexible, faster and it saves budget.

### What does guided self-service mean?

- **Author content** in a self-service capability
- **Offer a structured, simplified process** for authoring between reusable components and Veeva PromoMats for review and approval that is easy for end users to understand
- **Automates and simplifies steps** by integrating with Veeva, respecting MLR status from Veeva in Shaman

**Guided** means we add additional guardrails for content authoring:

| Phase | Guardrails |
|-------|------------|
| Pre-authoring | Provide design templates, possibly with locked elements |
| Authoring | Respect brand design system, limit brand colors, fonts, images, styling of buttons etc, add content cards, manage references |
| Post-authoring | Shaman compliance assistants check Quality of content, Language and compare against previous approved content (MLR compare) |

Key capabilities:
- Reuse approved components across channels
- Maintain traceability between content, references, and approvals
- Export content compliantly to execution systems (Veeva CRM, SFMC, web, etc.)

### Veeva Vault PromoMats Integration

Shaman is deeply integrated with Veeva Vault PromoMats for:
- MLR (Medical, Legal, Regulatory) review and approval
- Version alignment between authoring and approved assets
- Export of approved content for downstream activation
- Import of Components (images), Claims, references and modular content during authoring

The Prepare feature can be toggled on in the Shaman Menu settings before syncing the presentation to Veeva. If it is used in combination with the Brand Stories feature, the user will be able to choose between Brand Stories, slides, or a mix of both when preparing for the meeting.

In order to prepare for a meeting, the presenter must first open the presentation and select an account. Once this has been done, the Connect menu can be opened, and the Prepare tab can be found under the Profile section.
â€‹
NB:

â€‹
