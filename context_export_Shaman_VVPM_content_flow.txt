# MLR Documents | Emails

Path: Admin Space > Approved Email Builder

Overview
MLR reviews are a key practice in pharmaceutical marketing, ensuring that marketing and promotional materials comply with regulatory standards. The MLR process usually begins after the client approves the marketing creative work. A team of legal, medical, and regulatory experts carefully reviews each marketing component, assessing the accuracy of medical information, adherence to legal requirements, and compliance with ethical marketing practices.
Although the MLR process may vary slightly among pharmaceutical companies, the primary goal remains the same.

In this article:

What is MLR document creation in Shaman?
MLR document creation for approved email in Shaman is designed to further simplify the process of creating multi-channel content for Veeva Vault.
An MLR document is a combination of a visual rendition of your email content, and the MLR proof you receive that approves the distribution of the email content you are creating for a specific amount of time and to a specific audience. Without an MLR document, it is not possible to approve content in Veeva Vault and make it available for your end users to users.
We understand that not all of the Shaman users in your organization will have access to Veeva Vault, so we have enabled a tool in Shaman that allows all users to create an MLR document in Shaman and sync their email fragments or templates directly to Veeva Vault (based on the email and your configuration).
The workflow outlined below means that Shaman users only need to contact their local Veeva Vault admin once in the process - to approve the MLR document for distribution and also approve the content (email fragments, email templates, or template fragments) in Veeva Vault.
In this article we will take you through step by step how to create an MLR document, and then sync your content to Veeva Vault.
How to create an MLR document in Shaman and sync your content to Veeva Vault?

What is MLR document creation in Shaman?
MLR document creation for approved email in Shaman is designed to further simplify the process of creating multi-channel content for OnCore Vault.
An MLR document is a combination of a visual rendition of your email content, and the MLR proof you receive that approves the distribution of the email content you are creating for a specific amount of time and to a specific audience. Without an MLR document, it is not possible to approve content in OnCore Vault and make it available for your end users to users.
We understand that not all of the Shaman users in your organization will have access to OnCore Vault, so we have enabled a tool in Shaman that allows all users to create an MLR document in Shaman and sync their email fragments or templates directly to OnCore Vault (based on the email and your configuration).
The workflow outlined below means that Shaman users only need to contact their local OnCore Vault admin once in the process - to approve the MLR document for distribution and also approve the content (email fragments, email templates, or template fragments) in OnCore Vault.
In this article we will take you through step by step how to create an MLR document, and then sync your content to OnCore Vault.

An introduction to MLR documents for Marketing Email content
Although Marketing Email templates and dynamic content blocks are not synced and distributed via Veeva Vault, it is still important to be compliant and consistent with every Email that reaches your HCPs. Whilst there is no relation between Salesforce Marketing Cloud and Veeva Vault, there should still be proof that a document has been through the MLR review process.
An MLR document is a combination of a visual rendition of your email content, and the MLR proof you receive that approves the distribution of the email content you are creating for a specific amount of time and to a specific audience.
In Shaman, you can easily and quickly create MLR documents for both Marketing email templates and dynamic content blocks. Let's take a closer look.
How to create an MLR document for Marketing Email content in Shaman
Once you are ready to create an MLR document for a Marketing email template or dynamic content block, click the ‚ÄúMLR‚Äù tab.
From here, you can export a PDF of your email template or dynamic content block to then send for review and approval, in case you use a different system than Veeva Vault for MLR review and approval.
Export PDF options
Include responsive design (mobile view): Enabling this option will include a rendition of the mobile version of your email design in the same PDF export as the desktop version.
Compare against previous PDF version: If you need to resubmit your content for approval, and want to include a comparison against a previous version (for example; one that was returned during review, or a previously approved version) you can easily do so by enabling the toggle.
Include document information: Enable this toggle to include the metadata of the Email document in the PDF export.
Good to know!
If you are exporting a Marketing email template, you will see an additional option appear underneath the ‚ÄúPDF file‚Äù option: ‚ÄúInclude email dynamic content‚Äù
You can click this toggle to include dynamic content blocks in the exported PDF file, and approve the campaign as a whole.
To export your PDF, you can either download directly or click "Send to Email" - your PDF export will be in your Email inbox within a few minutes.
You will see two options available in the section "MLR document in Veeva Vault".
Create new: select this option if you wish to create an MLR document in Shaman
Link to existing: select this option if you wish to link to an existing MLR document that is readily available in your Veeva Vault.
Click "create new" to begin the process. You will then be redirected to a page where you can add all the details for your MLR document:
Name*: This field is automatically populated from the name of the corresponding email content you are creating the MLR document for. You can edit the name by simply typing in this field. This is a mandatory field.
Description: This field is automatically populated from the description of the corresponding email content you are creating the MLR document for. You can edit the name by simply typing in this field. This is an optional field.
Brand*: This is a dropdown menu, automatically populated with the brand assigned to your email template or dynamic content block. This is a mandatory field.
Language*: This is a dropdown menu, automatically populated with the language assigned to your email content. This is a mandatory field.
Country*: This is a dropdown menu, automatically populated with the country assigned to your email content. This is a mandatory field.
Expiry Date*: Using the calendar which opens when you click on this field, you can select the expiry date of the MLR document. This is a mandatory field.
